About

Hello, my name is Jessica Bauer and I am switching careers to become a data analyst. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Arts (English Literature, UW-Madison) and recently returned to school to develop the skills required to be a successful analyst. I have experience with analyzing data using Excel and SQL as well as employing Tableau for exploratory data analysis with visualizations and constructing dashboards and storyboards.

Currently I work as a Health Unit Coordinator in an ICU at the teaching hospital in Madison, WI. I believe my background in English and experience working in a healthcare setting, where I tackle exacting demands and apply precise communication in a high-pressure environment, complement my skills as an analyst and grant me the ability to comfortably work with a wide variety of stakeholders and be able to clearly communicate my findings in audience-targeted presentations.

Curious by nature, I possess a love of solving puzzles. There is nothing quite like starting with a huge chaotic pile of pieces and then through dedication and methodical techniques watching them form a coherent and visibly appealing image. I look at data analysis the same way; gathering a large amount of data that is ineffective on its own and applying an analytical mindset and tools that turn it into a successful and visually informative story. I find considerable enjoyment being challenged by data and revealing its patterns.
